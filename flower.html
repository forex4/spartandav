Flower

Flower is a 2D world builder containing a large number of different blocks. So that your worlds are not boring, I decided to add several types of mobs here. In order to build an ordinary house, you need logs, nails, etc. In order to grow a tree, you need a seedling and a lot of time. Here, at Flower, you can create the world of your dreams, without resorting to physical resources. Flower is perfect for those who like to create landscape designs, build unique structures - in a word, for creators.
