Free Photo Stock

I really enjoy taking photos and sharing my photos with other people for free. Previously, I used photo stocks such as Pexels, Pixabay and Unsplash for this. But for some reason Unsplash is gradually becoming paid, and photo stocks Pexels and Pixabay have completely stopped working in my country.

Therefore, without thinking for a long time, I decided to make my own free photo stock on Scratch, which I called Free Photo Stock. Here are my best photographs, which I allow you to use for free and without attribution. Let high-quality photographs be available to everyone!

Instructions

The Free Photo Stock program is needed for navigation and quick search of photos. It contains thumbnails of photos in poor quality (for quick work) and links to the original images, which are stored on Google drive. In order to use the Free Photo Stock program, you need to click on the "Open in browser" button below.
