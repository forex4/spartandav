Ship Battle

Ship Battle is a very fun game that you can play with your friends online or with bots. Here you need to shoot small cannonballs at your opponents. These nuclei, in contact with water, form a large funnel into which the opponent must fall.

I got the idea for Ship Battle when I accidentally found this project: https://scratch.mit.edu/projects/73644874/, for which I am very grateful to the author.



Screenshots




Instructions

To control your ship you need to use the right and left keys or the A and D keys. To rotate the cannon you need to use the up and down keys or the W and S keys. To shoot, use the spacebar. Click on the "Open in browser" button below and start playing right now in your browser.
